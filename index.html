🚨 Active Recall Issued May 2026 · UK Office for Product Safety & Standards
Joolz Aer2 Car Seat Adapter Set
Product ref: 2605-0189 · Serial numbers starting NL311- · Sold June 2025 – May 2026
Affected brand
Joolz
Sister brand · Bugaboo Group
Safety risk
High
Affected products
All NL311- units
Resolution
Full refund
What happened: The Joolz Aer2 Car Seat Adapter Set may fail to properly engage and lock with the spring-loaded pin on the Aer2 stroller chassis. The adapter can appear secure but may be incorrectly locked — if it detaches during use, the car seat can tip forward and fall to the ground with the infant inside.
Affected products: All Joolz Aer2 Car Seat Adapter Sets with serial numbers beginning NL311-, sold between June 2025 and May 2026. The Joolz Aer2 stroller itself is not affected and can continue to be used safely without the adapter.
Consumer action: Stop using the adapter immediately — even if it appears properly attached. Customers will receive a refund email within 2 weeks with a form link and a prepaid return label. Do not attempt to continue using the adapter while awaiting the refund.

Ongoing
Refund process underway. Joolz sending prepaid return labels. Resolution timeline: 2 weeks from recall notification.

Bugaboo Giraffe Highchair
18,280 units affected · Sold May 2023 – April 2025 · Class action lawsuit active
Affected brand
Bugaboo
Strollers unaffected
Units recalled
18,280
Injury reports
13 minor
Legal status
Class action active
What happened: The screws securing the front legs of the Bugaboo Giraffe highchair can loosen or fall out during normal use, allowing the front legs to fully detach from the chair. Bugaboo received 22 reports of detachment, including 13 reports of minor injuries to children.
Class action dispute: A class action lawsuit alleges that Bugaboo's recall notice placed the blame for the defect on consumers, stating the legs could detach "if the screws were not fully tightened during setup." The lawsuit claims this framing was inaccurate and that the fault lies with the product design, not consumer assembly.
Stroller range not affected: This recall is specific to the Giraffe highchair product line. All Bugaboo strollers — Fox, Donkey, Butterfly, Dragonfly, Bee, Ant, Kangaroo — are unaffected and safe to use.

Class action still active and circulating on parent forums and legal news sites. No public settlement or resolution announced.
